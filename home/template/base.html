Adopt a Seat Program


As a result of the COVID-19 pandemic that has affected so many organizations, we now have a cap to the amount of students that can fill each classroom. To be able to adhere to health and safety guidelines as well as being able to keep our doors open, we came up with a solution- the Adopt A Seat program. Through this program, donors will be able to pay a small portion of what would have been the tuition of an enrolled student. Help us keep being able to teach and care for the children of Atlanta by donating today.
